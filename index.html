About

Amine Zidi

Founder & Editor, Societas

I am a high school student at Lycée Lyautey in Casablanca, in my final year with specialisations in Economics and Social Sciences and Mathematics. Societas was born out of a simple and genuine desire: to share what I care about with the people around me, my classmates and friends and perhaps with any curious visitor who happens to find these pages.

"Knowledge only means something when it is shared. This site is my way of doing that."

My interests are broad and sincere. Politics in all its forms: the balance of power, the ideas that shape the world and the decisions that change lives. Cinema, which I consider one of the most powerful ways to understand a time, a society or a human condition. Sport, especially football and tennis, which have taught me as much about strategy, character and competition as any classroom. And law, which gives the structures of the world their clearest shape.

I do not claim to be an expert. I am someone who observes, reads and thinks and who believes that putting words to what you understand about the world is a worthwhile discipline, whether you are a student, a professional or simply a citizen. Societas is where I practise that.
